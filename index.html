FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 1–2 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and month-end dates. However, we often accommodate last-minute and same-day bookings — just give us a call.
Are you fully insured?
Yes. We carry £10m Public Liability Insurance and comprehensive Goods-in-Transit insurance on every move. Your belongings are fully protected from the moment we pick them up.
Do you provide packing materials?
Yes! We supply boxes, bubble wrap, wardrobe boxes, and all other packing materials. These can be included in your quote or purchased separately. We also offer an eco-friendly reusable crate option.
Can you handle pianos and antiques?
Absolutely. We have specialist equipment and trained staff for handling heavy, delicate, and high-value items including grand pianos, antique furniture, artwork, and marble.
Do you offer storage solutions?
Yes, we offer flexible short and long-term storage in our secure, climate-controlled London facilities. Storage is available from just one week upwards, with easy access and competitive monthly rates.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfer, credit/debit card, and cash. A small deposit is required to secure your booking, with the remainder due on moving day. We never charge hidden fees.
